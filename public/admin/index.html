Menu Editor
Open the editor →

Invited to edit? The first time, open the invite email from Pages CMS (check your spam) and click the link inside it — that's how editors sign in.
“Sign in with GitHub” is for the site owner only.

How to make changes
  1. 1Change a price — open a section, click the item, edit Price, then Save.
  2. 2Rename an item — click the item and edit its English or Arabic name, then Save.
  3. 3Add an item — open a section, click + under Items, fill in the name and price, then Save.
  4. 4Remove an item — click the trash icon on that item, then Save.
  5. 5Change a photo — open a section, open Section photo, use Upload photo, then Save.

Your changes appear on the live menu about 1–2 minutes after you Save.
Tip: bookmark the editor page to jump straight back in next time.
